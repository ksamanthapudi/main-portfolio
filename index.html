Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Kavya Samanthapudi, a CS graduate and an aspiring Data Analyst with a strong foundation in data analysis, data engineering, and business analytics. I have good knowledge of coding in languages like Python and SQL, and hands-on experience working on projects involving ETL pipelines, data visualization, and business intelligence. My expertise includes building efficient data pipelines and developing interactive dashboards that facilitate data-driven decision-making. At Synergina, I collaborated with cross-functional teams on a healthcare client’s Patient Management System, where I enhanced data workflows using AWS S3 and Redshift to improve processing times and system performance. I also developed real-time dashboards in AWS QuickSight, providing valuable insights for healthcare administrators to make informed decisions. In my virtual work simulation at The Forage, I analyzed employee turnover data and delivered actionable insights through comprehensive reports. I created a Tableau dashboard to visualize key metrics, helping HR teams improve retention strategies and streamline decision-making processes. Currently, I am pursuing a Master's in Computer Science at Pace University. I have also worked on academic projects like COVID-19 Vaccine Distribution Optimization and Customer Churn Analysis, where I applied data-driven techniques to address real-world challenges. My cloud experience includes working with AWS services such as S3, RDS, and Redshift, focusing on data integration and processing. I am passionate about leveraging data to solve complex problems and am dedicated to continuously learning new technologies in data science and analytics. I am a strong communicator and team player, eager to contribute to projects that drive business innovation and growth through data.
